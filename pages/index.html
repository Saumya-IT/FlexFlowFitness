What we offer

Hatha Yoga

Experience the balance of body and mind through traditional Hatha Yoga postures and breath control techniques.

Vinyasa Flow

Flow seamlessly from one pose to another, connecting breath with movement in our dynamic Vinyasa Flow classes.

Yin Yoga

Deeply relax and release tension in your connective tissues with our slow-paced and meditative Yin Yoga sessions.

Kundalini Yoga

Restorative Yoga
